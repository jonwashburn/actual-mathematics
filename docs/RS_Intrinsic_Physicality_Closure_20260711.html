THEOREM, RS-INTERNAL

Physicality without an external permission slip

The classical split was wrong. Recognition Science does not place mathematics on one side and matter on the other, waiting for a laboratory observer to connect them. A recognition process is physical already. The formal closure now executes generated δ arithmetic inside one fixed J-cost process instead of accepting an arbitrary “physical world” from a caller.

First-principles brief

Why

The earlier theorem made empirical acceptance part of the definition of physical truth. That imported a classical boundary RS has removed.

Who

Researchers using Actual Mathematics and RS who need a machine-checked statement of what it means for generated arithmetic to be physically executed.

Goal

Map every empty-ledger generated δ theorem into one nontrivial process with fixed recognition steps and the forced reciprocal cost.

Success

A Lean theorem with no empirical premise, no caller-supplied Peano world, a fixed process carrier, exact J observable, decoy failures, and an axiom-clean build.

Constraints

Lean is the judge. The theorem must preserve the checker receipt, reject collapsed carriers, and avoid claiming a named instrument or our universe realizes the formal process.

What the system can now do

Generate a state. Start at ratio 1. The fixed base-two witness multiplies the current state by 2, producing 1, 2, 4, 8, and onward.
Compare states physically. The reciprocal cost J(x/y) is zero exactly when two process states are identical. The fixed base-two first step has cost 1/4.
Run arithmetic on that process. Addition and multiplication are reconstructed by primitive recursion over the unique generated trace, with zero and successor laws proved.
Execute any generated theorem. rs_generated_closure proves every GeneratedTheorem true in the fixed J-orbit process for every variable assignment, while retaining the original checker equation.

Hidden-premise questions

  1. What was wrong with the old framing? It promoted a finite measurement receipt from correspondence evidence into the source of physicality.
  2. Could this still be a renamed copy of natural numbers? A bare equivalence would be. Here successor is separately proved to be multiplication by the selected base 2, and J decides state identity inside formula satisfaction. The process structure is doing work.
  3. What is the strongest reason the new framing could still be wrong? Calling every formal J-orbit “our physical world” would repeat the reach error. The theorem closes RS-internal execution, not a named experimental realization.
  4. What is the smallest artifact that delivers the capability? One Lean module: ActualMathematics/Physicality/RSDeltaClosure.lean.
  5. Which facts are checked? Peano generation, successor multiplication, J separation, J uniqueness under named laws, an inhabitant satisfying every cost law, recursive arithmetic, J-bearing satisfaction, checker preservation, and theorem transport.
  6. Which premise remains explicit? J uniqueness uses the proved reciprocal, normalization, composition, order, and calibration laws. jcost_law_witness proves that J itself satisfies all five, so the law class is not empty.
  7. Does J uniquely force the generator 2? No. Two is the concrete witness fixed by this realization. The theorem proves this world works; it does not prove that no other faithful base works.
  8. What would create motion without progress? Building a hardware counter, wrapping ℕ in Fock vocabulary, or adding an empirical receipt without putting the process operation in the theorem path.
  9. What must survive representation changes? The generated orbit, identity state, step operation, J equality test, arithmetic recursion laws, and satisfaction of the transported formula.
